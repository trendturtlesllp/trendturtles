About TrendTurtles

Quantitative Trading Technology Company developing and operating systematic trading systems for proprietary trading

Who We Are

TrendTurtles is a quantitative trading technology company that develops and operates systematic trading systems for proprietary trading. Our systems are developed, validated, deployed and continuously improved for broker prop desks through quantitative research, AI-assisted analysis, cloud infrastructure and disciplined risk management. Our trading technology is actively deployed within the Indian proprietary trading ecosystem. At the same time, continuous research and validation drive the development of additional models for domestic and international markets.

What We Do

Every trading system begins with research, not assumptions. Market data, quantitative research, statistical analysis and AI-assisted workflows are used to generate, evaluate and validate trading ideas before they reach live deployment. Once validated, our systems execute predefined rules consistently without discretionary decision-making, allowing every strategy to operate exactly as designed.

Our Approach

We believe consistent performance comes from disciplined processes rather than prediction. Every component of our platform—from research and validation to execution and risk management—is designed to improve reliability, reduce human bias and continuously adapt through evidence-based learning.

Continuous Research

Financial markets evolve continuously, and so do our systems. Research never stops after deployment. Existing models are continuously monitored, refined and evaluated, while new strategies for domestic and international markets undergo ongoing research, testing and validation.
